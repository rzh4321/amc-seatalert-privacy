Privacy Policy for AMC Seat Checker

Last updated: February 18, 2025

Information We Collect

AMC Seat Checker collects and stores:

  • Email addresses provided by users for seat availability notifications
  • Seat numbers that users want to monitor
  • Movie showtime information associated with the notification requests

How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for:

  • Sending notifications when selected seats become available
  • Managing user notification preferences
  • Ensuring proper functionality of the notification service

Data Storage

User data is stored securely in our database and is automatically removed when:

  • The movie showtime has passed
  • A user unsubscribes from specific seat notifications
  • A user unsubscribes from all notifications for a specific showing

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute your personal information to any third parties.

Data Deletion

Users can request removal of their data by:

  • Clicking on the unsubscribe link for specific seat notifications in the automated email.
  • Clicking on the unsubscribe link for specific movie showings in the automated email.
  • Contacting us directly for complete data removal

Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy at any time. Users will be notified of any changes through the extension or via email.
